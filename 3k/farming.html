Definition of farming noun from the Oxford Advanced Learner's Dictionary

farming

noun
 
 
/ˈfɑːmɪŋ/
 
/ˈfɑːrmɪŋ/
[uncountable]
jump to other results
  1. the business of managing or working on a farm
    • to take up farming
    • organic farming
    • sheep/fish/salmon farming
    • modern farming methods
    • a farming community
    • the British farming industry
    see also battery farming, dryland farming, factory farming, gold farming, mixed farming, strip-farming, truck farming
