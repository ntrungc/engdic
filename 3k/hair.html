    • a hair salon

     [uncountable] hair growing on the body of a person or an animal
    • body/facial/pubic hair
    see also camel hair, horsehair
  3.  
     [countable] a piece of hair from a person or an animal
    • There's a hair in my soup.
    • The rug was covered with cat hairs.
    • She pushed a stray hair behind her ear.
  4. -haired
     (in adjectives) having the type of hair mentioned
    • dark-haired
    • long-haired

Idioms
get in somebody’s hair
  1. (informal) to annoy somebody by always being near them, asking them questions, etc.
a/the hair of the dog (that bit you)
  1. (informal) alcohol that you drink in order to make you feel better when you have drunk too much alcohol the night before
hang by a hair/thread
  1. (of a person’s life) to be in great dangerTopics Dangerc2
keep your hair on
  1. (British English, informal) used to tell somebody to stop shouting and become calm when they are angry
let your hair down
  1. (informal) to relax and enjoy yourself, especially in a lively way
    • It’s about time you let your hair down and had some fun!
make somebody’s hair stand on end
  1. (informal) to shock or frighten somebody
    • a chilling tale that will make your hair stand on end
not harm/touch a hair of somebody’s head
  1. to not hurt somebody physically in any way
not have a hair out of place
  1. (of a person) to look extremely clean and neat
not see hide nor hair of somebody/something
  1. (informal) not to see somebody/something for some time
    • I haven't seen hide nor hair of her for a month.
not turn a hair
  1. to show no surprise, shock, etc. when something unusual or unexpected happens
    • She listened to his confession without turning a hair.
split hairs
  1. to pay too much attention in an argument to differences that are very small and not importantTopics Opinion and argumentc2
tear your hair (out)
  1. (informal) to show that you are very angry or anxious about something
    • She's keeping very calm—anyone else would be tearing their hair out.
    • I felt like tearing my hair out in frustration.
