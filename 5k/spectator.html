Definition of spectator noun from the Oxford Advanced Learner's Dictionary

spectator

noun
 
 
/spekˈteɪtə(r)/
 
/ˈspekteɪtər/
jump to other results
  1. a person who is watching an event, especially a sports event
    • The new football stadium will hold 75 000 spectators.
    • Thousands of spectators lined the route of the royal procession.
