Definition of tenant noun from the Oxford Advanced Learner's Dictionary

tenant

noun
 
 
/ˈtenənt/
 
/ˈtenənt/
jump to other results
  1. a person who pays rent for the use of a room, building, land, etc. to the person who owns it
    • They had evicted their tenants for non-payment of rent.
    • The decorating was done by a previous tenant.
    • tenant farmers (= ones who do not own their own farms)
